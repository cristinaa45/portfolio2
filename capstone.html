Capstone Project

Research and campaign planning about the company Inbeat.

About Inbeat

Inbeat is a marketing company that helps brands grow by working with micro-influencers. These are smaller creators who make honest, natural, and relatable content. Micro-influencers are known for higher engagement and more trust from their followers compared to big celebrity influencers. That’s why many brands choose Inbeat to connect with audiences in a more authentic way.

Inbeat also provides tools for influencer discovery, content creation, and ad optimization. Brands looking for fast growth on social media platforms especially TikTok and Instagram use Inbeat to streamline their marketing. You can learn more directly from their website: Visit Inbeat.co

Who Their Audience Is

Inbeat’s audience includes small businesses, medium sized companies, marketing agencies. These businesses want more visibility online and need influencers to showcase their products in a real way. Many of these brands don’t have time to contact creators one-by-one, so they rely on Inbeat’s search tools and creator lists to speed up the process.

Their typical customers want to grow on TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube. They look for content that feels natural, fun, and trustworthy—not overly perfect ads.

How Inbeat Works

Inbeat has one of the biggest influencer search engines available online. Brands start by searching for creators based on location, follower count, niche, or style. Then they can save influencers, contact them, and create campaigns.

Inbeat also offers creative services, where their internal team produces TikTok ads, UGC videos, and paid social campaigns. They track analytics such as views, engagement rate, click-through rate, and conversions.

A more detailed description of their services can be found here: Inbeat Agency Services

What They Do

  • Create TikTok and Instagram ads
  • Grow followers, views, and engagement
  • Produce UGC (user-generated content)

  • Manage influencer partnerships and outreach

Audience Research

My Research Questions

  • What do small businesses look for in influencer marketing?
  • What social media platform do people trust the most for product recommendations?
  • Do micro-influencers help brands grow faster than celebrity influencers?

Primary Research (Survey)

I conducted a survey with 25 students and young adults. Here are the results:

  • 70% said they trust small influencers more than celebrities.
  • 60% said they discover new brands on TikTok before Instagram.
  • 80% said they prefer simple, real videos over polished ads.
  • 65% said they buy something after seeing a TikTok review.

Secondary Research (Articles, Trends, Competitors)

According to marketing studies, micro-influencers have higher engagement rates than larger influencers. Trend reports from Influencer Marketing Hub show that “authentic content” performs significantly better.

Sources used:

  • Influencer Marketing Hub
  • Later: Micro-Influencer Trends
  • AspireIQ (competitor)

Key Insights

  • People trust small creators more because they feel relatable.
  • TikTok is the #1 platform for discovering new brands.
  • Short, real videos outperform long, scripted ads.
  • Businesses want tools that help them find influencers quickly.
  • Authenticity is more important than follower count.

How This Influences My Campaign

My campaign will focus on TikTok videos created by micro-influencers. The content will be simple and natural, not overly edited. I will highlight trust, honesty, and product reviews because that is what the audience responds to the most. The campaign will also use UGC-style videos, since they perform well with Gen Z and young adults.
