Capstone Project

My research on the company I chose: Inbeat.

About Inbeat

Inbeat is a marketing company that helps brands work with influencers. They use small creators, also known as micro-influencers, to make real and honest content. Brands use Inbeat to reach people in a more natural way.

Who Their Audience Is

The audience for Inbeat is mostly businesses that want to grow online. These businesses need help finding influencers and making good social media posts. Inbeat also helps companies who want more people to see their products on TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube.

How Inbeat Works

Inbeat has a big database of influencers. A brand can go on their website, search for the type of creator they want, and pick the ones that fit their style. Inbeat also helps create ads and tracks how well the content performs. Everything is simple so companies can grow faster.

What They Do

They help brands:

  • Find influencers
  • Create social media ads
  • Grow followers and views
  • Make content that feels real
